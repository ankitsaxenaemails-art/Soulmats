How to use this bible
Each mat has a different buyer.
Each buyer needs a different entry point.

The framework is constant. The avatar, the dominant emotion, the inciting incident, and the symbol energy are specific to each mat. Every ad is built from Phase 1 research — not guessed, not templated.

Each mat has 3 ads: Cold (Problem Aware) · Warm (Solution Aware) · Retargeting (Product Aware)

Cold — Problem Aware
Permission Hook
Does not mention the mat until 55–65% through. Builds emotional weight first. Blame-shifts before the product appears. Avatar: never heard of Soulmats.
Warm — Solution Aware
Skeptic Validation Hook
Opens by mirroring their skepticism. Validates past failures. Introduces the mechanism as why this is different. Avatar: seen an ad, still on the fence.
Retargeting — Product Aware
Symbol Energy Hook
Speaks to the symbol directly. "Your symbol is waiting." Short, specific, assumes the relationship exists. Avatar: visited site, did not purchase.
